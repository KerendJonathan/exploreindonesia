Tradional Products

Traditional Wayang

Wayang Indonesia is a traditional performing art in the form of a theater of leather puppets or wooden puppets. It is performed with light, with sound and distinctive movements.

Tradional Spices

Herbs and spices have made a huge impact on cuisine since time immemorial. Spices enhance the flavor and aroma of dishes, and come straight from Indonesia.

Traditional Foods

Indonesian food is a culinary delight that captivates travelers. You'll find delicious and diverse dishes from different regions, such as the iconic fried rice, satay, and sate and other foods...

Industrial Goods
